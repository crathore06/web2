A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1932 - 2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainlabdeen Abdul Kalam 15 Octomber 1931- 27 july 2025 was an indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of india from 2002 to 2007. he was served as the 11 th president of india from 2002 to 2007. he was born and raised in Rameswaram Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. he spend the next four decades as a scientist and science administrator, mainly at the defence research and development.
